DBS FinTech Mini Hackathon

CashFlow Shield

A judge-ready RM copilot that detects SME cash-flow stress, explains the cause, and routes the next action through responsible lending guardrails.

Judge critique

The first version was useful, but not yet winner-grade.

Too soft

It had a good story, but weak quantified impact. Judges could ask: how does DBS measure success?

Too abstract

The AI score appeared from nowhere. Judges need data signals, model logic, and confidence.

Too risky

FinTech judges will worry about auto-lending, explainability, false positives, and suitability.

Problem

SME liquidity issues are predictable, but intervention is often late.

RM workload

Relationship managers must manually inspect account activity, invoices, and fixed-cost timing.

Customer pain

SME owners usually ask for help after payroll, supplier, or receivable pressure has already escalated.

Bank risk

Late support can increase default risk, reduce retention, and create unsuitable credit pressure.

Solution

A human-reviewed workflow, not an automatic credit engine.

  1. Read consented operating-account and invoice timing signals.
  2. Score liquidity buffer, receivables concentration, fixed-cost pressure, volatility, coverage, data quality, and policy readiness.
  3. Generate RM actions with policy gates and suitability checks.
  4. Track pilot outcomes: review time, early outreach, and customer resolution.

Market grounding

The product uses public benchmarks as context, not as credit approval.

  • EnterpriseSG EFS-WCL terms anchor responsible working-capital constraints.
  • SingStat/MAS tables provide SME and business-loan market context.
  • Borrower-specific decisions still require DBS policy, affordability checks, and RM review.

Why this wins

It balances innovation with banking realism.

  • Innovation: proactive cash-flow intervention before SME stress becomes default risk.
  • Quality: narrow workflow, visible data signals, explainable risk drivers, and policy guardrails.
  • Desirability: helps SMEs get earlier support while making RM work faster and safer.
